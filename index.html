What this is —
and isn't.

We are a small practice. We can't offer free full engagements, grant programs, or subsidized retainers at scale. What we can offer is real: free resources you can use today, and a genuine willingness to talk with nations and nonprofits who are trying to make sound technology decisions without someone trying to sell them something.

If a conversation leads to paid work, great. If it doesn't, that's fine too. The resources are yours regardless.
